Technology Education and Research Integrated Institutions, commonly known as TERii was founded under the aegis of Gyankund Foundation Trust in the year 2007. TERii is among Top Engineering and Management college in Kurukshetra . The trust Gyandkund Foundation has a clear motto “To Educate and To Serve”. TERii as an institution was established with a fortune mission of working for the development of the society by spreading education and delivering highly qualified engineers, entrepreneurs and managers to the society. Situated at the Kaithal Road in Kurukshetra, TERii campus is spread in a lavishing 20+ acres of green land which symbolizes peace, prosperity and optimism. The campus includes state of the art infrastructure, laboratories and workshops equipped with modern equipments, library, cafeteria, hostels and much more.TERii facilitates students from pan India, Nepal and other countries across the globe as well. The campus symbolizes a global diaspora of cultures wherein students from all across the globe live and study together . The real essence of the mixture is observed in the outcomes of projects delivered by the students and the events organized by these students.
From the initial phase, the founders of TERii decided to make TERii a self-finance and the best engineering and management college of India.

The founders had a clear and well defined mission– to build an unaided, self– financed Institute committed to deliver education of internationally recognized standards, an Institute that would go on to serve as an intellectual resource base in India and the sub-continent.
